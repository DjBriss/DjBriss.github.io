Welcome

Brisson Sheet Metal is a contractor in the installation and fabrication of duct work, for heating and cooling purposes. We are ready to help you with all your construction projects, from building a new house to renovations. Contact us today!

Who we are

Brisson Sheet Metal was founded by Norman Brisson in 1996, and he built his shop in 1997 in Casselman, Ontario. He had to expand the building multiple times to accomodate the demand, as well as more tools and equipment. Norman is assisted by his two employees, Kevin and Randy. It will be their pleasure to help you out!

Our services included:

Free estimates

Custom fabrication and installation of ventilation systems

Fabrication of other sheet metal products

Achievement of any of your projects !
